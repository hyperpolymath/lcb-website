## Governance

- Decisions are made via **lazy consensus** (silence is approval).
- Major changes require **approval from at least 2 maintainers**.
- New maintainers are added by **unanimous approval** of existing maintainers.

## Expectations

- Maintainers are expected to:
  - Respond to issues/PRs within **7 days**.
  - Review and merge contributions in a **timely manner**.
  - Follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Adding New Maintainers

1. Nominate a contributor who has:
   - Demonstrated expertise in the project.
   - Consistently contributed high-quality PRs.
   - Shown commitment to the project's goals.
2. Existing maintainers vote. Approval requires **unanimous consensus**.
