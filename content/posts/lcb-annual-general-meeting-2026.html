The NUJ London Central Branch Annual General Meeting will take place on Thursday 24 April 2026 at 7pm. This is the most important meeting of the branch year, at which officers are elected, the branch accounts are presented, and motions are debated.

AGM Agenda

  1. Apologies for absence
  2. Minutes of the 2025 AGM
  3. Chair's report on branch activity
  4. Treasurer's report and audited accounts
  5. Election of branch officers for 2026-2027
  6. Motions to the branch
  7. Motions to NUJ Annual Delegate Meeting
  8. Any other business

Standing for Office

Nominations are open for the following branch officer positions:

  • Branch Chair
  • Vice-Chair
  • Branch Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Equality Officer
  • Health & Safety Officer

To nominate yourself or another member, contact the branch secretary at least 14 days before the AGM. All nominees must be current NUJ members attached to the London Central Branch.

Submitting Motions

Motions for debate at the AGM must be submitted in writing to the branch secretary at least 7 days before the meeting. Motions should be clear, concise, and include a proposer and seconder.
