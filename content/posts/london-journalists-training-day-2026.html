The NUJ London Central Branch is pleased to announce a Training Day for London journalists on Saturday 22 March 2026. The day will feature practical workshops covering skills that are increasingly essential for working journalists.

Workshops

Morning Sessions (10am-12:30pm)

  • OSINT for Journalists: Open-source intelligence techniques for investigative reporting. Learn to verify sources, trace digital footprints, and use publicly available data responsibly.
  • Data Journalism with R and Julia: Hands-on introduction to data analysis and visualisation for news stories.

Afternoon Sessions (1:30pm-4pm)

  • Digital Security for Journalists: Protecting sources, encrypted communications, secure file handling, and threat modelling.
  • Rights and Contracts: Understanding your rights as a journalist, negotiating contracts, and dealing with copyright issues in the age of AI.

Registration

The training day is free for NUJ members. Non-members are welcome at a rate of £15 per session. Lunch will be provided.

Places are limited. Register by contacting the branch secretary or via the members area on this website.
