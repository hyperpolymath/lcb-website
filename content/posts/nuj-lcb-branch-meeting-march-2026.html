The NUJ London Central Branch will hold its monthly branch meeting on Thursday 12 March 2026 at 7pm. The meeting will be held in hybrid format, with an in-person venue at the NUJ head office and a BigBlueButton video link for remote attendees.

Agenda

  • Minutes of the previous meeting
  • Branch officer reports
  • Motion: Support for freelance rate transparency campaign
  • Guest speaker: Media law update from the NUJ legal team
  • Any other business

All NUJ members attached to the London Central Branch are welcome to attend. If you are not yet a member, you can join the NUJ and request attachment to the London Central Branch.

The BigBlueButton link will be emailed to members 24 hours before the meeting. Check your inbox or contact the branch secretary if you have not received it.
